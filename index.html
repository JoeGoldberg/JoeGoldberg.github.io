Control-M Workbench

Why Workbench

Control-M Workbench provides services needed to build, debug and test Control-M workflows. With this facility, you can build complex applicaiton automation that:

  • Controls the date and time your applicaiton is scheduled
  • Determines the flow by defining how one component or executable relates to others via predecessor/successor relationships
  • Checks suuccess or failure to determine if the next steps should be performed or not
  • Automatically captures output and makes it available for human vonsumption such as problem determination
